The Problem

One ladder. One language. Millions left behind.

Placeholder — Colonial education built a single ladder to success, labeled in English. 75 years after independence, that ladder has not changed. Brilliant students who think, dream, and reason in Bengali are told — implicitly, structurally — that their language is not good enough.
