Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st June 2026

4sh Foundation ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information (collected via the Join Us form):

  • WhatsApp number
  • Email address
  • How you can help (volunteer / donor / follower)

1.2 Usage Information:

  • Interactions with the website
  • Device information such as IP address and browser type (via Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about volunteering or community updates.
  • To personalise your experience and suggest relevant content.
  • To communicate updates, news, and mission-related information.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4sh Foundation does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers for operational purposes (e.g. hosting, analytics, form processing).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Data Retention

Personal data is retained as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccuracies.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational constraints.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use Google Analytics to enhance user experience and analyse usage patterns.
  • You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

This website may include links to third-party websites or services. 4sh Foundation is not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Data Security

4sh Foundation uses industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

4sh Foundation does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

4sh Foundation reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
